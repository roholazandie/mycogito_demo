Introduction

Welcome to MyCogito! This is the first digital platform to give people's data back to them. It gathers all your data (with your permission of course) for you in one place. It analyzes your digital data and gives you a holistic understanding of who you are while respecting your privacy. The analysis is not used to sell you products or bombard you with advertisements. On the contrary, we believe in empowering our users by giving them scientific high-level analytics that has been withheld from them by big companies.
We believe in the age of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data users can benefit from technologies instead of being exploited by them. Feel free to navigate through the app and see different analytics. Some parts may not work properly at the moment but we are glad to hear your feedback. (All the data here is fake and for demonstration purposes)
